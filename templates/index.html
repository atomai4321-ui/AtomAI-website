About AtomAI

An AI-driven search engine for materials that don’t exist yet.

AtomAI isn’t a static database like Materials Project — it’s a screening engine. XGBoost and Random Forest models rank millions of A₂BB′O₆ compositions for stability and band gap, and DFT validates the most promising candidates before synthesis.

This explorer is the first public interface to that pipeline: type in four elements, and AtomAI tells you whether a Double Perovskite Oxide is known, predicted, or doesn’t exist in the current search space — and why.

Research Focus

Photocatalytic Remediation & Green Hydrogen Production

Environmental Remediation

Screening DPO photocatalysts for dye and antibiotic degradation under visible light, guided by predicted band gaps and band alignment.

Green Hydrogen

Identifying photoelectrochemical water-splitting candidates ranked by predicted stability and band gap.

ML Prediction

XGBoost and Random Forest models screen millions of compositions, ranking candidates by predicted stability and band gap before any synthesis begins.

Experimental Validation

DFT calculations and lab characterization — XRD, Raman, UV-Vis, FESEM, XPS — confirm that predicted materials perform as expected in reality.
