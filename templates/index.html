About AtomAI

Search Beyond Existing Materials

AtomAI goes beyond traditional materials databases by predicting new Double Perovskite Oxides using AI. Machine learning rapidly screens millions of compositions, while DFT validates the most promising candidates before synthesis.

01 · AI-Based Property PredictionPredict stability, bandgap, and photocatalytic activity of double perovskite materials using machine learning.
02 · Curated Materials DatabaseOrganize validated predictions into a structured, searchable database for rapid access and comparison.
03 · Interactive Search PlatformSearch and explore materials by composition, predicted properties, and AI insights through an intuitive interface.
04 · New Materials DiscoveryIdentify promising, previously unexplored double perovskite candidates for DFT validation and experimental synthesis.
